What We Do

If you want to have a fun time on the water in Lake Havasu call us today for all your watercraft needs. We do all the launching and retrieving so you can sit back and relax. We want to be your choice for all your rental needs. Stop by and see our large selection of tri-toon boat's-pontoon boats-deck boats-wakeboarding boats-jet boats, and jet skis (PWC) We only rent the cleanest and well maintained equipment. From our boats to our toys, you will not be disappointed in the selection. Come and hang out with friends and family and enjoy the lake. The recreational possibilities are endless and so is the FUN.

We are conveniently located minutes from Windsor Beach and the London Bridge. We also deliver to Parker, most campgrounds and to Pirates Cove. We have all the necessary equipment to make your day on the lake a great experience with professional assistance.
